UNDERSTANDING THE FITNESS FUNCTION
----------------------------------
Fitness in DRQ measures both survival and dominance. From the paper:

  Fitness(w_i) = SUM over timesteps of: (N/T) * (A_i / SUM_j A_j)

Where:
  - N = number of warriors in battle
  - T = total simulation timesteps (80,000)
  - A_i = 1 if warrior i is alive at timestep, 0 otherwise

This rewards warriors that:
  1. Survive as long as possible
  2. Eliminate opponents to increase their share of the reward

Maximum possible fitness = N (if warrior survives alone for entire battle)
